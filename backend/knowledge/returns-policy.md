# Returns policy

A return means sending a product back to us before a refund is issued. A refund is the money credited back to your account after we receive and inspect the return.

To start a return, contact support with your order ID within 30 days of delivery. We will send a prepaid return label by email within 1 business day.

Pack the item in its original packaging where possible. Items returned without packaging may be subject to a 10% restocking fee.

Once we receive the item, inspection takes 1 to 3 business days. You will receive a confirmation email when the refund is approved and processed.

Digital items and opened consumables are not eligible for return.
