# Accessibility

Our website meets WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards.

If you need assistance placing an order, our support team can take orders by phone or live chat Monday to Friday, 9 am to 6 pm EST.

Product packaging includes braille labels for the product name and key safety information on all items.

If you have a visual impairment, screen readers are fully supported on our website. Contact us if you encounter any barriers.

We offer large-print instruction manuals for all products free of charge. Request one by contacting support with your order ID after purchase.

If you require an accessible format for invoices or product documentation, contact support and we will provide it within 2 business days.
