# Address change

You can update your delivery address before your order is dispatched.

To change the delivery address, contact support with your order ID and the new address. Address changes are only possible while the order status is pending or processing.

Once the order has been handed to the courier, address changes must be made directly with the carrier. We will provide the tracking number and carrier contact details.

If a package is returned to us due to an incorrect address, we can reship it at no extra charge. Contact support with your correct address and order ID.

For future orders, update your saved addresses in Account Settings to avoid this issue.
