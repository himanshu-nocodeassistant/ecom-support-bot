# Smart home hub guide

The smart home hub connects up to 50 devices and supports Wi-Fi 6, Zigbee, and Z-Wave protocols.

Setup takes about 10 minutes. Download the companion app, plug in the hub, and follow the on-screen pairing instructions.

The hub requires a 2.4 GHz or 5 GHz Wi-Fi connection. It does not support enterprise Wi-Fi networks or captive portal logins (e.g. hotel Wi-Fi).

Voice assistant compatibility: works with Amazon Alexa, Google Home, and Apple HomeKit.

If the hub shows a red LED, it has lost its network connection. Press the reset button on the back for 3 seconds to reconnect.

Firmware updates are automatic when the hub is connected to the internet. Do not unplug the hub during an update (indicated by a pulsing blue LED).

The hub comes with a 12-month warranty. The USB power adapter is rated at 5V 2A.
