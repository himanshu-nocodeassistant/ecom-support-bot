# Payment methods

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express credit and debit cards. We also accept PayPal and Apple Pay at checkout.

All payments are processed securely via Stripe. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.

Bank transfers are available for orders over $500. Contact support to request a bank transfer invoice.

We do not accept cash, cheques, or cryptocurrency.

If a payment fails, the most common causes are insufficient funds, an incorrect billing address, or a card blocked for international transactions. Try updating your billing address or contacting your bank before retrying.

Refunds are returned to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days of approval.
