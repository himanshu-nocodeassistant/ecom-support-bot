# Subscription billing

Some products are available on a monthly subscription that gives you a 15% discount compared to one-time purchases.

Subscriptions are billed on the same day each month, starting from your first order. You will receive an email reminder 3 days before each billing date.

To pause a subscription, go to Account Settings → Subscriptions and select Pause. You can pause for up to 3 months.

To cancel a subscription, select Cancel in Account Settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You will not be charged again after cancellation.

If a subscription payment fails, we will retry after 3 days and then after 7 days. If payment still fails, the subscription is paused and you will be notified by email.

Subscription items ship on the same schedule as standard orders and follow the same refund and return policy.
