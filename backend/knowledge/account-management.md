# Account management

You can create an account at checkout or from the sign-in page. An account lets you track orders, save addresses, and view purchase history.

To change your email address, go to Account Settings and verify ownership of the new email with a confirmation link.

To reset your password, click Forgot Password on the sign-in page. A reset link is sent to your registered email and expires after 30 minutes.

To delete your account, contact support. Account deletion removes your personal data within 30 days in accordance with our privacy policy. Order history required for tax or warranty purposes may be retained for up to 7 years.

Guest checkout orders cannot be accessed after purchase without an order ID and the email used at checkout.
