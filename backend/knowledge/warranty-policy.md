# Warranty policy

All hardware products sold in our store come with a 12-month manufacturer warranty from the date of purchase.

The warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. It does not cover damage caused by accidents, misuse, unauthorised modifications, or wear and tear.

To make a warranty claim, contact support with your order ID and a description of the defect. We may ask for photos or a short video showing the issue.

Approved warranty claims result in either a free repair, a replacement unit, or a full refund at our discretion.

The blender motor and the headphone speaker drivers each carry a separate 6-month extended warranty on top of the standard 12 months.
