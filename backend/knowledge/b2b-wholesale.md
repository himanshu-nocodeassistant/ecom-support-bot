# B2B and wholesale orders

We offer volume discounts for businesses purchasing 10 or more units of the same product.

Discount tiers:
- 10 to 24 units: 10% off
- 25 to 49 units: 18% off
- 50 or more units: 25% off

To place a wholesale order, contact our B2B team by email at b2b@support.example.com with your company name, VAT number, and a list of the products and quantities you need. We will send a custom invoice within 2 business days.

B2B orders ship on the standard schedule. Lead times for very large orders (100+ units) may be longer.

Net-30 payment terms are available for verified business accounts. Apply through the B2B portal on our website.

B2B orders follow the same return and warranty policy as consumer orders.
