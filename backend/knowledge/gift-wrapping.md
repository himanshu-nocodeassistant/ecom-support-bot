# Gift wrapping

Gift wrapping is available for all products at a flat fee of $3.99 per item.

To add gift wrapping, select the Gift Wrapping option on the product page or in your cart before checkout. You can include a personalised message of up to 150 characters at no extra cost.

Gift-wrapped orders are shipped in a sealed box with a printed message card. The price tag is removed from all gift orders.

Gift wrapping cannot be added after an order is placed. If you forgot to add it, contact support within 1 hour of ordering and we will do our best to accommodate the request.

Gift wrapping is not available for large or oversized items.
